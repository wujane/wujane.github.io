MGMT 295A — Entrepreneurship and Venture Initiation

UCLA Anderson MBA

Price Center for Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Upcoming: Fall 2026
Past sessions: Winter 2023, Spring 2024, Winter 2025, Fall 2025

Q+A on why EVI is about giving you the confidence to innovate

Class description

One in three of you will be your own boss at some point — and that doesn't include those who will join, invest in, or advise startups. Whether you already have a pitch, a side hustle, or an idea, this class provides a research-backed toolkit to turn an idea into an impactful company. The course includes case discussions, lectures, guest speakers, and a course-long startup project.

MGMT 274A — Tough Tech Commercialization Capstone (ATMC)

UCLA Anderson MBA (2nd year, 20 spots)

Class description

Anchored in innovation and technology strategy, this capstone blends academic rigor with practical application. Students analyze cases across space, biotech, AI, and more, and develop commercialization strategies addressing technology, market, regulatory, scale, and financing risks.
